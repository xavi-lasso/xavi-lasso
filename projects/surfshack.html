Surfshack

A hangout place inspired by sustainable living, board sports, and easy-listening underground house music.

An idea that mixes up a few things that I have very close to my heart became the perfect reason to explore the procedural design and real-time Houdini - Unreal Engine graphics pipeline.

Process

Privileged to be born in Ecuador, a land full of diverse nature, I brought inspiration from the Galapagos Islands, a beautiful beach called Tortuga Bay. Using Mapbox, I pulled map data from the island, and processed it with Houdini's Heightfield tools to generate texture maps and scattered points to instance vegetation with procedural art direction capabilities.

Interior design and architecture to me are places I go to find calm and balance. Inspired by 3D Printing techniques and sustainable architecture, I designed a procedural pod generator, using curved shapes and a solid body. The practice was perfect for understanding fundamentals of procedural modeling.
