Xavi Works

I am a crafter in the field of audio/visual arts. On every collaborative or personal project I enjoy allowing for the project's needs to determine how the output expresses itself. A play between intuition, fun, and design are at the core of my process. I have an affinity for music, 3D, and creative technology. My practice takes inspiration from mother nature, environment art, subcultures, and the feeling of connection to the now.

Please feel free to reach out, we can have a chat over some coffee or hang out in the virtual space.

Procedural 3D design, motion, art direction, real-time visuals, music production.
